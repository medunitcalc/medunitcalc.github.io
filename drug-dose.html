Drug Dose (mg/kg)

Drug Dose (mg/kg) is a method to calculate the correct dose of a medication based on a patient’s weight.

Formula

Dose (mg) = Weight (kg) × Dose per kg (mg/kg)

Example

Patient weight = 70 kg
Recommended dose = 2 mg/kg

Total Dose = 70 × 2 = 140 mg
Interpretation: Administer 140 mg to this patient

Clinical Notes

  • Always check drug reference for the correct mg/kg dose.
  • Adjust for renal or liver impairment if necessary.
  • Round dose appropriately based on formulation available (tablets, vials, etc.).

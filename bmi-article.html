How to Calculate BMI Correctly

Body Mass Index (BMI) is one of the most commonly used methods to assess body weight in relation to height...

The BMI Formula

BMI = weight (kg) ÷ height² (m²)

Step-by-Step Calculation

  • Measure weight in kilograms
  • Measure height in meters
  • Square the height
  • Divide weight by squared height

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: < 18.5
  • Normal weight: 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: 25 – 29.9
  • Obesity: ≥ 30

Common Mistakes

Many users calculate BMI incorrectly by using centimeters instead of meters or forgetting to square the height.

About MedUnitCalc

MedUnitCalc is an educational website designed to provide medical calculators, tools, and educational articles for healthcare professionals, students, and enthusiasts.

Goal & Audience

  • Goal: Make medical calculations easy, accurate, and accessible.
  • Audience: Students, doctors, nurses, and anyone interested in medical education.

Features

  • BMI Calculator
  • Mean Arterial Pressure (MAP)
  • PaO₂ / FiO₂ Ratio
  • Glasgow Coma Scale (GCS)
  • Anion Gap (AG)
  • Creatinine Clearance (CrCl)
  • Drug Dose (mg/kg)

Our Mission

To provide reliable, clear, and easy-to-use medical tools online so that healthcare professionals and students can make informed decisions faster.
